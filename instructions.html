Machine format

The transitions are entered as quintuples of the form State Sep Char Sep State Sep Char Sep Move where

  • State can be any nonzero alphanumeric character
  • Sep is any nonzero sequence of commas or whitespaces
  • Char is any character that does not match Sep
  • Move is one of '>', '<' or '!'

The meaning of the quintuple (q, c, q', c', m) is do define the transition "when in state q and looking at character c, transition to state q', writing character c' and moving in direction m".

Different transitions have to be separated by at least one whitespace character and the last one has to be followed by at least one whitespace character as well
